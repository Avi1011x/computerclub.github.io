WHAT IS THE UOM APPCUP?

UoM AppCup is an inter-university mobile app competition open to all universities in Mauritius. The competition aims to foster an environment of innovation, creativity, and collaboration among the participants. This event is an ideal platform for aspiring mobile app developers and designers to showcase their talent and gain recognition

  • The UoM AppCup is a mobile app development competition open to all University students of Mauritius.
  • Participants will compete in teams to create the best mobile app that incorporates the themes of ESG, or Environmental, Social, and Governance factors.
  • Winners of the UoM AppCup will receive cash prizes, certificates, T-shirts and other goodies from our sponsors, as well as the opportunity to showcase their app to industry professionals.

THEMES

Our theme for this year's competition is ESG, which stands for Environmental, Social, and Governance factors, where the spefic themes are PRESERVATION OF INDIGENOUS SPECIES, CITIZEN SAFETY, LAW AND COUNSELING with respect to ESG. We are calling all the talented individuals to showcase their skills and create innovative mobile applications that address the issues surrounding climate change, social justice, and corporate responsibility. The ESG theme is more than just a challenge, it's an opportunity for participants to make a positive impact in the world while showcasing their skills and creativity. CRITERIA

In the first quest, you must prove your team's worth by crafting a compelling MOBILE APP PROPOSAL that aligns with the competition themes. Your PROPOSAL will be evaluated by a panel of expert judges, who will determine if your team has what it takes to advance to the final challenge. Once you have completed this initial quest, you will be one step closer to claiming victory in the UoM App Cup.

PATRTICIPATION CRITERIA

  • To participate in UoM AppCup, students must be registered at one of the universities in Mauritius.
  • The competition requires teams to have a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 5 registered students to compete.
  • Participants are required to bring their laptops and other equipment to develop their mobile apps during the competition.
  • Basic knowledge of programming is necessary to participate in the competition

SELECTION CRITERIA

  • Project Proposal on a chosen theme(1-2 pages) include your team name, university, and all the members' names
  • Clear description of your idea
  • Tools and Technology you will use, e.g., frameworks
  • Explanation on how you will implement AI in their app

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • IMPLEMENTATION OF AI
    How effective the Implementation of AI was integrated within the proposed solutions.
  • FRONTEND
    The goal is to render the entire interface elegant, easy to use, fast, and secure.
  • BACKEND
    The aim is to develop a back end system that prioritizes efficiency, speed, and security in its design and implementation.
  • JUDGE's REQUIREMENT
    Participants must ensure that they incorporate the judge's requirement, which will be announced on day 1, into their App.
