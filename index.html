Pick’Em

It’s that time of year again… FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™ is fast approaching and it’s time to put your football knowledge to the test with World Cup’s Pick’Em! Pick'Em is a game of predictions and upsets: make your picks for which teams will win and lose, earn points, and fight your way to the top of the University’s Leaderboard! Leaderboards will let you join up and compete against all of your friends. cores, winning teams etc for the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™. Points will be calculated and updated after each phase.

Based at the university of Mauritius, Computer Club has taken the challenge to provide a platform for students to develop the technical and soft skills that will later help them in their work environment. We organized several talks, events, seminars, workshops, etc on different areas of Information Technology. We helped students improve their interpersonal skills as well as soft skills. After successfully leading the European Football Championship as UEFA EURO 2020 Predictor which amassed a total of 150 participants, the computer club is back with another promising event and this time hoping to break numbers.

How does it work? →

  • 1st phase: Group Stage

    For the Group Stage, it’s up to you to correctly predict the order that teams will finish in each group. Do you think Brazil will stomp through their group? Go ahead and choose them wisely above everyone else. The choice is yours. Group Stage will be open for picks at the starting phase of the competition.

    How are points awarded? Correctly predicting the order of the first two teams in the group will earn you 5 points each. Correctly predicting the order of the last two teams in the group will earn you 2 points each. Max points: 5+5+2+2

    Example: Let’s take for example Group G. Let us assume that Brazil came 1st, Switzerland 2nd,Cameroon 3rd, Serbia 4th.

    The input of a participant: 1. Brazil✅ +5

    2. Cameroon❌ +0

    3. Switzerland❌ +0

    4. Serbia ✅ +2

    Points earned: 7

  • 2nd phase: Knockout Stage

    Once the Group Stage has finished, you'll be able to pick the winners for matches at each step of the way throughout the Knockout Stage -- and ultimately settle on who you think will take home the 2022 World Cup title. Check back after the Group Stage wraps up to make your predictions for the Quarterfinals and beyond.

    At this stage you will have to predict the winning team and correct scores of each match that will be played.

    How are points awarded? Perfect score will earn 5 points. For e.g., a player predicts Belgium will beat England by 1-0 and the actual score is 1-0.

    Correct score margin will earn 3 points. For e.g., a player predicts Belgium will beat England by 1-0 and the actual score is 2-1 or 3-2.

    Correct team winner will earn 2 points. For e.g., a player predicts that France will win the match but fails to meet the correct margin (predicts 1-0 and actual score 2-0).

    Deadline Last 16: Saturday 3rd December. Two hours before kick-off.

    At this stage you will have to predict the winning team and correct scores of each match that will be played.

    Correct score margin will earn 3 points. For e.g., a player predicts Belgium will beat England by 1-0 and the actual score is 2-1 or 3-2.
